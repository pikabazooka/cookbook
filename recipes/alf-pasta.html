steps

  1. salt and pepper the chicken breast and cook it on a large skillet. 8mins per side, remove from skillet and chop into cubes.
  2. add chicken broth/water to skille
  3. add the butter and cream to the skillet
  4. simmer over low heat for 5-10 minutes
  5. whisk in the garlic, italian seasoning, salt, pepper and simmer for one minute
  6. whisk in the parmesan cheese until melted
  7. serve immediately
